Hello and Welcome to Daz Max Bridge.
	
Please find the Wiki on the project page on GitHub: https://github.com/sebjf/dazmaxbridge/wiki

To install:

1. Copy the 'bin' folder to your 3DS Max Installation Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\Autodesk\3ds Max 2014\)
2. Copy the 'plugins' folder to your Daz Studio 4 Installation Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\DAZ 3D\DAZStudio4\)

You are done.

Enjoy Daz Max Bridge!
